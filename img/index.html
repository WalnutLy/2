We are Media Arts and Technologies

We are a community of learners focused on design, media arts and technologies.

We love graphic design, web design and development, digital imaging and illustration, interactive media design, print publishing, video and animation.

We strive to develop solid concepts, design relevant projects, work collaboratively, communicate effectively, and solve problems, all while learning the appropriate software, tools and techniques to achieve these ends.
